When in the project, if you are on a phone (only tested on android), you can tilt the platform by tilting the phone, or apply force through the platform by moving it around. If you are on a computer, or the orientation sensor is not accesible, mouse input will automatically be enabled, and you can move the platform with the mouse. Mouse can be disabled with space bar!
